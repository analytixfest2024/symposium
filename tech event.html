TECHNICAL EVENTS

1. Code Machine

• 2 members per team
• Maximum of 2 teams per department can participate in this event.
• There will be 3 rounds.
• Questions are set at a level ranging from basic to intermediate.
• Internet access is not allowed.
• Selection will be based on the solving speed and success rate of test case.
• Judges decisions are final.
• Required Skill : C, C++, Java, Python, Data structures and algorithms.

2.Byte Battle

• 2 members per team.
• Maximum of 2 teams per department can participate in this event.
• Round 1 : you have to solve 20 questions in 20 minutes.
• 7 teams will be qualified for the second round .
• Instructions for the second round will be revealed on the spot.
• Internet access is not allowed.
• Any irregularity will result in immediate disqualification of your team.
• Required Skill : Python, web technology.

3. Code Decode

• 2 to 5 members per team(minimum 2 required*).
• Maximum of 2 teams per department can participate in this event.
• Participants are allowed only from computer science background.
• There will be 3 rounds.
• Questions are set at a level ranging from basic to intermediate.
• Your team will be disqualified, if any one of you use smart devices.
• Required Skill : Python, SQL, Data Science Basics.
