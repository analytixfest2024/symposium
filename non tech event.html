NON TECHNICAL EVENTS

1.Treasure Hunt

• Maximum 3 members per Team.
• Maximum of 2 teams per department can participate in this event.
• You have to find the treasure(one Piece) within the given time.
• Participants should use only one phone during the entire event.
• Participants should have any QR Code scanner application.
• The reason for scanning the QR code will be revealed on the spot.
• If the participants or team use the open sources to find the answers during the event that team will be disqualified.

2. Extraction

.• 2 members per team.
• Maximum of 2 teams per department can participate in this event.
• There will be 2 rounds, those who are getting high points in the first round will be qualified for the next round
• In the First round, A picture will be displayed on the screen , the participants should analyse the picture, Connect the picture and find the answer within 15 seconds.
• Second round is based on buzzer system. Buzzer application will be revealed on spot.
• Mobile Phones are allowed with good internet speed.

3.Mindsnap Data Puzzle

• 2 members per team.
• Maximum of 2 teams per department can participate in this event.
• In the first round you have to observe the images for 20 seconds and you'll be given 10 seconds to recall.
• points will be given for every correctly recalled items.
• Top 7 teams will be qualified for round 2.
• In the second round, you have to observe the images again and you have to recall it in order.
• points will be given for every correctly recalled items.
• Selected teams will be qualified for round 3.
• In the third round, a video will be played and questions will be asked relevant to that video. You have buzz first or raise your hand to answer.
• Incorrect answer gives opposing team a chance.
• Team with Highest total points will be declared as the winner.
