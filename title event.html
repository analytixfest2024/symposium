TITLE EVENT

Startathon

About the Event:
• Startathon is a technical event where participants must devise innovative solutions for a given problem statement.
• They will choose one problem statement out of three options provided by the organizers.
• Participants are required to prepare a presentation, either a PPT or a poster, outlining their problem, innovation, solution, techniques, and technologies they intend to use.

Instructions :
• Register your team through the provided registration portal.
• Once registered, you will be added to a whatsapp group named “Hackathon”.
• Add your team name and members names in the group.
• On march 6, problem statements will be delivered via whatsapp group after inauguration.
• Work on your presentation during the designated time and be ready to present in the afternoon.
• Presentation time : 5 - 8 minutes.
• Submit your presentation before 2:15 pm.
• No late submission will be accepted.
• Judging will be based on the idea, presentation, creativity and orginality of the content.

Rules :
• Each team must consist of 2 or 3 members.
• Use of AI tool is permitted.
• Each team should bring their own laptop with internet access for the presentation.

Requirements:
• Laptop with internet access for the presentation.
